# Frontend Interview Preparation Roadmap (3+ Years Experience)

## Goal

Prepare for frontend interviews from basic to advanced level without focusing on coding problems initially.

Focus Areas:

1. JavaScript Fundamentals & Internals
2. Browser & Web Fundamentals
3. React Deep Dive

5. Performance Optimization
6. Behavioral & Experience-Based Questions

### Do

- Discuss tradeoffs
- Mention performance considerations

### Avoid

- Memorized definitions only
- Overcomplicated explanations
- Jumping directly to solutions

# Final Advice

At 3+ years experience, interviews are less about syntax and more about:

- Understanding tradeoffs
- Explaining architecture decisions
- Debugging ability
- Performance thinking
- Communication
- Real-world experience

Deep understanding of fundamentals usually matters more than memorizing hundreds of questions.
